Introduction

JJF Phonebook ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our contact management service.

Our Commitment: Your contacts are your private data. We act as a responsible custodian and never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Info

Name, email, password

Contacts

Names, phones, emails, companies

Profile Details

Job title, address, birthday

Notes

Custom notes and tags

2.2 Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the service (features used, time spent)
  • Device Info: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Log Data: Error reports, performance metrics
  • Cookies: Authentication tokens, preferences

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing Service: Storing and syncing your contacts across devices
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage to enhance features and performance
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse
  • Communication: Sending updates, security alerts, and support responses
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share limited data only in these specific situations:

3.1 Service Providers

Trusted third parties who assist in operating our service (hosting, analytics, customer support). They are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

3.2 Legal Requirements

If required by law or valid request from public authorities (e.g., court order, subpoena).

3.3 Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred. You'll be notified via email and prominent notice.

We never share your contacts with advertisers or third-party marketing companies.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security to protect your data:

  • Encryption: AES-256 for stored data, TLS 1.3 for transmission
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization
  • Monitoring: 24/7 threat detection and logging
  • Backups: Encrypted daily backups with limited retention
  • Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing

While we strive for perfect security, no method is 100% guaranteed. You're responsible for maintaining your password's confidentiality.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe, CCPA in California), you may have:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive data in structured, common format
  • Opt-out: Object to certain processing
  • Withdraw Consent: At any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise rights, email privacy@jjfphonebook.com or use dashboard settings.

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication (keeping you logged in)
  • Security (CSRF protection)
  • Preferences (saving your settings)

Optional analytics cookies (with consent) help us improve. You can manage cookie preferences in browser settings.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where we operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses) for international transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or dashboard alert. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects recent revisions.
